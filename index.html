Abstract

To begin interacting with research papers:

  1. Check out the pre-loaded foundational academic papers in the left sidebar.

This tool runs 100% in-browser. Your papers and API keys are stored only locally in your session and are sent safely and directly to the secure Google Vertex/AI Studio APIs.
